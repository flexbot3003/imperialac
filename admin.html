Launch checklist

Complete these checks before publishing the final policies.

  • Confirm the club’s Information Officer and official privacy contact details.
  • Verify the final list of hosting, database, font, analytics and media providers.
  • Remove references to services that are not used and add any that are missing.
  • Set up a secure process for access, correction and deletion requests.
  • Use clear photography notices at events and obtain appropriate permission for identifiable minors.
  • Review the policies after major website, club or legal changes.
  • Have the documents reviewed by a South African legal or POPIA professional before launch.
