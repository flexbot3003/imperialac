What we are building now.

Mission

Disciplined competition.

To build a disciplined and competitive senior football team through structured coaching, honest management and clear standards.

Vision

Sustainable growth.

To become a respected and sustainable Pretoria football institution, expanding only when the foundations are ready.

Club principles

How Imperial behaves.

  1. Arrive prepared, communicate early and respect the time of others.
  2. Earn trust through consistency, not promises.
  3. Compete hard without disrespecting teammates, opponents or officials.
  4. Take responsibility for mistakes and respond with improvement.
  5. Protect the badge in public, online and inside the dressing room.
  6. Help teammates grow and never make the club smaller for personal gain.
  7. Training standards must match matchday ambitions.
  8. Selection is earned by performance, discipline and commitment.
  9. Confidence should create courage, not arrogance.
  10. Leave Imperial stronger than you found it.
